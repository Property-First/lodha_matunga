ABOUT THE PROJECT

Lodha Divino

Lodha Divino, located on Bhaudaji Road in Matunga East, Mumbai, is a premium high-rise residential development offering luxurious living in one of the city’s most well-connected neighborhoods. The project features G+38 storey towers with G+10 levels of parking and an expansive 35,000 sq.ft. open recreational area, combining modern architecture with large green spaces, world-class amenities, and high-end finishes for a refined lifestyle.

Strategically positioned, it offers excellent connectivity with just 1 minute to Eastern Express Highway, 15 minutes to BKC, and 20 minutes to Lower Parel, along with close proximity to top schools, colleges, dining spots, and lifestyle hubs. The project offers spacious 2, 3, and 4 BHK residences with pricing starting from approximately ₹3.98 Cr++, making it an ideal choice for luxury homebuyers seeking comfort, connectivity, and premium living in central Mumbai.
